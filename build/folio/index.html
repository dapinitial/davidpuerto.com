About me

David Puerto

UI Designer & Developer From Seattle, WA USA

With over a decade of web experience, David Puerto has established a career through the mastery of meaningful, high-impact, interactive media design. Dedicated to creating refined, yet innovative ecosystems, David Puerto synthesizes complex workflows into extensible UI pattern libraries with an underlying design language. Experienced in web-based screen design and the modern web development toolchain; he prides himself as a seasoned, hands-on UX leader that delivers the highest-level of fit and finish.
