Corporate Redesign

This was my first legit, major contracting gig after leaving the full-time world.  I joined a team of personnel on site at their Cleveland, OH headquarters and began to mesh with a team of internal and external professionals to overhaul sherwin.com.  What a fun experience with a great team!

I was contracted to assist with user experience design for a revitalization of their corporate website.  Soon after starting the project, which had a rough start, the leadership changed, and I was folded into front-end development to assist in the responsive presentation layer.  I also acted as a liaison for the company, while on contract, to travel with employees, and meet with ad agencies to help communicate the company vision and refine our development processes.  This project was a success.

View it live at: http://www.sherwin-williams.com/

February 8-9, 2015 / Seattle, WA
